Our Mission Statement

eAIrth aims to document, analyze and communicate the impacts of artificial intelligence (AI) on Earth.

eAIrth Governance

eAIrth is a collective of independent researchers. An advisory group comprised of university faculty, staff, students, alums and community members advises eAIrth.

eAIrth Project

eAIrth is currently undertaking two pilot research projects. The first is an Infrastructure Acknowledgement based out of the Spatial Analysis Lab (#300 International Center). The second is a case study of Google’s data centers in Chesterfield County.
